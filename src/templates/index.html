About Us

Hello! We're a club at Sir Winston Churchill Secondary. We aim to bring awareness to free software licenses and to the software that we as a society choose to use. We also are a group of people who have an interest in general technology, or who are programmers ourselves.

We plan to make software that is useful or that could help empower our club members. We believe in a society where everyone has their voice heard and where people share knowledge so that we may all benefit.

We plan to teach people about UNIX like operating systems such as GNU/Linux and the BSD operating systems. Every month, we plan to have a ricing competition to compete for the best looking laptop! Other project ideas are too good to share right now, but once we do them we'll put them on this site.

About this site

This site was designed to be minimal, with less than 120 lines of css, and no javascript. To avoid using javascript, we used a static site generator that Preston Pan, the president of this club, wrote specifically for their websites.

If you like that this website has no hidden trackers, google fonts, or really anything that could undermine your privacy, or frankly, your eyes, consider supporting us. You can do this by checking out the websites of all our members and maybe donating to them, or by simply sharing our blog posts and projects around.
